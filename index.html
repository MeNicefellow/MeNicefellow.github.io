About Me

Hello, I'm Dr. Nicefellow. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from a globally recognized university. My fascination with Large Language Models (LLM) and Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) is profound and enduring. My journey in the AI landscape is fueled by an insatiable curiosity and a passion for exploring the potential of these groundbreaking technologies.

I am an avid supporter of AllenAI, OpenAI, MetaAI, MistralAI, and 01-ai for their significant contributions to the AI field. In my opinion, ChatGPT is one of the most remarkable achievements in the history of AI.

My contributions to the AI field extend beyond academic research. I develop open-source tools that benefit the community, write technical articles highlighting important milestones, curate datasets for training LLMs, and fine-tune LLMs while making their weights accessible to the public.
